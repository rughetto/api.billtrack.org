Mr. ROBERTS (for himself and Mr. HATCH) submitted an amendment intended to be proposed by him to the bill H.R. 2, to amend title XXI of the Social Security Act to extend and improve the Children's Health Insurance Program, and for other purposes; which was ordered to lie on the table; as follows:

 Strike all after the enacting clause and insert the following:
SECTION 1. CHILDREN'S HEALTH INSURANCE PROGRAM REAUTHORIZATION ACT OF 2007 (CHIPRA II).
 The text of the Children's Health Insurance Program Reauthorization Act of 2007 (H.R. 3963, 110th Congress) as passed by the Senate on November 1, 2007, is hereby incorporated by reference.
